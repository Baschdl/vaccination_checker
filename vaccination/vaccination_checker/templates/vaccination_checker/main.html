About Vaccination Checker


The Pfizer and SAP Vaccination Checker enables people all over the world to finally gather all their important vaccination information in one place. It provides a fine summary of all relevant points of your vaccination pass. Vaccines have never been this easy.


By uploading photos of your vaccination pass, this machine-lerning driven, data-first and secure application offers a stable and accurate representation of your vaccination process.
